## Design Context

### Users
Space enthusiasts, potential investors, and talent looking at a next-generation space exploration company. They arrive expecting credibility, ambition, and technical sophistication.

### Brand Personality
Bold, Visionary, Precise — evoking the vastness of space with the precision of engineering.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark mode exclusively — deep space blacks with accent glows
- **Visual tone**: Cinematic, immersive, minimal UI chrome — let 3D and motion speak
- **Typography**: Geist Sans for headings (tight tracking, bold weight), Geist Mono for data/stats
- **Color palette**: Deep navy/black base (#0a0a0f), cool blue accent (#4f8fff), warm nebula highlights (#c084fc, #f472b6), white text
- **References**: SpaceX launch pages, Apple spatial computing, NASA JPL mission pages
- **Anti-references**: Cluttered dashboards, stock photo hero banners, generic SaaS layouts

### Design Principles
1. **Immersion first** — 3D and animation are the content, not decoration. The hero IS the experience.
2. **Restrained palette** — Black canvas, one or two accent colors. Let light and motion create hierarchy.
3. **Cinematic pacing** — Scroll-triggered reveals, not everything visible at once. Earn attention.
4. **Technical credibility** — Monospace for data, precise spacing, no playful rounded corners.
5. **Performance is design** — Smooth 60fps animations. Janky = broken.
